Pricing questions

A few helpful details.

Need help choosing? Send us a message.

Is KiddieGPT free forever?

No. New families can begin with a free trial so they can explore the experience before choosing Monthly or Yearly access.

Do Monthly and Yearly include different features?

No. Both family plans include the complete KiddieGPT family experience. Yearly simply offers a lower effective monthly price and saves $29 compared with 12 monthly payments.

Can I cancel my family plan?

Yes. You can cancel from your account billing settings. Your paid access continues through the end of the billing period you already purchased.

What happens when the free trial ends?

You will be asked to choose Monthly or Yearly access to continue using the full KiddieGPT family experience.

How does school pricing work?

School pricing depends on the size and shape of your rollout. Contact us with your student group, timeline, and goals, and we’ll help map the right approach.

Can I request a refund?

Our current refund terms are described in the Terms of Service. For billing help, use our contact form.
