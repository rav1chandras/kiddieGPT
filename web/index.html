Study smarter, in the right order

Turn one lesson into a complete study session.

KiddieGPT guides kids from understanding to remembering to checking their confidence—so practice feels purposeful, not overwhelming.

Step 1 · Study Mission

Start by understanding the lesson.

Kids bring in the page or file they are already studying. KiddieGPT turns it into a focused mission, highlights what matters, and gives them space to ask for a simpler explanation before moving on.

  • Works from an active tab or local file
  • Adjustable challenge level
  • Read-first flow encourages real comprehension
Step 2 · Flashcards

Build recall without starting from scratch.

Once the lesson makes sense, KiddieGPT creates a focused set of flashcards from the same material. Kids can flip the direction, revisit tricky cards, and ask a follow-up when a term still feels fuzzy.

  • Generated from the lesson they just read
  • Term-to-clue and clue-to-term practice
  • Small card sets keep review manageable
Step 3 · Quiz

Finish with a confidence check.

A short quiz helps kids see what stuck and where they need another look. Instead of repeating everything, they can focus on missed ideas and turn uncertainty into a clear next step.

  • Questions based on the same study source
  • Visible progress keeps kids oriented
  • Review focuses attention on missed concepts

Good questions

What parents usually ask first.

Still wondering about something? Send us a message.

Is KiddieGPT just another chatbot?

No. It’s a guided learning extension built around schoolwork and reading. Kids can bring in the page, file, screenshot, or problem they’re working on, then explain, study, practice, or ask follow-ups.

Will it simply give my child the answer?

KiddieGPT is designed to prioritize understanding: it can break work into steps, offer hints, explain concepts, and keep final answers hidden until a child is ready to check their thinking.

What ages is it for?

KiddieGPT is designed primarily for kids ages 8–14. Parents choose the appropriate grade level so language, examples, and explanations are better matched to the child.

How do the parent controls work?

Parent settings are protected by a PIN and include grade level, content safeguards, usage boundaries, and activity visibility. Your child can use the learning tools without changing those settings.

Where can my child use it?

KiddieGPT works as a Chrome extension alongside the web. It can help with active pages, selected text, uploaded files, screenshots, pasted questions, and compatible phone scans.

How do we get started?

Visit the KiddieGPT app, create your parent account, choose your child’s grade level and safety settings, and start exploring the learning tools together.
